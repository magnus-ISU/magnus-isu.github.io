The party needs to figure out how to get around its disintegrating skin to damage it, be able to see the environment in order to plan and be useful, steel themselves against its terror (and its offers of power), defeat its faithful servants, manage to even fight such a monstrous flying avatar, avoid looking at it too long, survive its hellfire or prevent it from being made, prevent it from cancelling their magic with its sword... and then figure out how to bind it in one place long enough to actually kill it. But it could be done. For running this, make hard moves all over and telegraph all the information to the party so they know everything they're up against before they engage.
