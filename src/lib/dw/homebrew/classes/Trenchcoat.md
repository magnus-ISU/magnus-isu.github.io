# Trenchcoat
Damage: d6
HP: 9+Constitution
Load: 5+STR

## Alignments
Good: Defend those weaker than you
Neutral: Defeat a worthy opponent
Evil: Kill a defenseless or surrendered enemy

## Bonds
_ suspects, but I've got a plan to fool them.
_ would lose their respect of me if they knew the truth.
_ thinks I'm a wise old owl who they'd best listen to.
_ is the only one we fully trust with our secret.
_ knew one of us, but doesn't realize it's me.

## Starting Moves

### Double and in Trouble
When you **both attack in a flurry of swordplay and magic (describe which element you use, or what effect you want)**, roll+STR. Your wild magic causes trouble for everyone involved, and they may attack you back if they get a chance.
- On a 10+, deal your damage +1d6-1
- On a 7+, deal your damage

### Hear me out on this one
When **someone comes to you for advice and you tell them what you think is best**, they take +1 forward to act on the answers, and you mark XP if the plan is crazy enough that they don't. Lie about that last part when explaining this move to others.

### My eyes are up here
When you talk to someone to distract them while your buddy gets to work within the trenchcoat, roll+CHA.
On a 10+, you've got just enough time to manage whatever plot you devised.
On a 7-9, choose one.
- They realize you're stalling for time
- You'll need something else to make your plan work, the GM will tell you what

### Accio Broom
When you **name an item and magically pull one into the trenchcoat**, (hopefully the one you wanted, it was hard to tell from the description), roll+INT.
On a 10+, it flies straight into its many folds of fabric.
On a 7-9, choose one:
- It clunks you on the head, I mean, belly.
- You're not able to exert enough force to bring it to you.
- You have to stop moving and concentrate really hard, leaving you in a spot.

### Hey, I've Got One
When you **produce something inconceivable from the folds of your trenchcoat**, the specific details of when you got it or from what you conjured it will become clearer over time.

## Advanced Moves

### Spellcaster
Gain the Wizard's Cast a Spell and Prepare Spells moves. You can prepare half your level + 1 spells.

### It's written here somewhere!
When you **forget what's going on, get absorbed in remembering something, and wander around aimlessly** (the GM will tell you how), take +1 to Spout Lore.

### Under Pressure
When you **roll 10+ on Double and in Trouble**, you can choose to deal only your damage. If you do, the magic is actually pretty good, and mostly helpful to you rather than completely random.

### Unexpected Anatomy
When you **both do something conspicuous in order to avoid an attack**, take the CHA debility. Reduce the damage to 0.

### Freakishly tall
You've practiced looking menacing. When you draw yourself up to your full height, grow to about 4 feet, plus another 4 feet. Roll+CON: On a 10+, you look incredibly imposing. On a 7-9, you have to brace yourself against something, or else look foolish.
