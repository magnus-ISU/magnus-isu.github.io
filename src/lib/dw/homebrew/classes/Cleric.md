# Cleric
###### Hallowed Hold
When you *stand on sanctified, holy ground*, hold 1 Miracle. (Stolen from pontificating on the universe, and maybe that Bloodborne and Dark Souls and Elden Ring has churches all around)
###### Unleash Miracles
When you *hold Miracle and pray for a saint's miracle*, roll+WIS. On a 10+, it goes off. On a 7-9,
- Spend 1 Miracle
- -1 ongoing to Unleash Miracles until you prove yourself
- Your God demands something of you; or you call attention to yourself; or are put in a spot; or your connection is cut off and the Miracle has unexpected/lesser effects.
###### A Flame in the Dark
When you *find a place of ancient worship*, ask your God what you must do in order to sanctify it. They will respond with 1 of the following requests:
- You must pray for a few hours here
- You must redeem someone before the holy site
- You must dedicate something to your God here
- You must repair something which time or infidels have defaced
When you have done so, hold 2 Miracles.
