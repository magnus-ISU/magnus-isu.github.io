# Illithid Parasite
The peoples of the Earth scramble around it, searching for meaning in their short lives. They shall not find it. They do not understand true joy is to commune with the Elder Brain; they cannot see.
You are not so weak. You are not so empty. You have left the womb, created by the Infinite Mind to seek out power and increase it's strength. You will force them to see, partake in the joy as their last miserable act before they relenquish their meaningless lives to be reborn.
Reborn with power. Reborn with purpose, and meaning. Reborn as you truly are; illithid and finally, at long last, beautiful like nothing else can be.
###### Stats
Damage: d8
HP: 8+Constitution
Load: 8+STR

## Backgrounds
###### Servant of the Deep
You are a parasite, burrowing deep within your hosts brain. No matter who they were, it is unimportant. You serve the Brain.
When you **leave your hosts brain to devour another mind**, you gain control of their body. You get hazy flashbacks from their mind; take +1 forward (once) to *Spout Lore* based on the knowledge from their memories.
If you are harmed outside of a host, you die.
###### Cultist of the Mind
You have long searched for this power, but after being granted it, you have found a way to control yourself and the parasite within, preventing it's consumption of you.
When you meet another possessed of psychic ability, roll+INT. Choose 2/1:
- Cut off their ability to access 
- Give them a command they must follow
###### Infected under Control
Before this horror began, you were another man. It threatens to consume you; you will not let it.
Choose one starting move from another class.
